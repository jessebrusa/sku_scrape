Product Description

The Collector 320’s compact and lightweight design keeps your favorite .223/.556, rimfire, airgun, or crossbow nimble and responsive, and the Collector’s capabilities are optimized for use at the ranges these platforms are normally used. This unit boasts a fast 60Hz frame rate for smooth operation and industry-best, outstanding visual acuity, based on Armasight’s Iron Wolf thermal technology platform. An extensive suite of features includes video and image capture, USB video stream, USB-C interface for viewing your recordings and photos on external devices such as computers or televisions. The Collector 320 offers multiple color palettes, intuitive simple 3 button controls, multiple reticle types and colors, and long battery life.

  • Image Detail Enhancement: Improved image clarity for precise target identification.
  • 1/2 MOA Boresight: Offers accurate and repeatable zeroing for dependable performance.
  • Standard Picatinny Mount Included: Ensures compatibility with standard mounting systems.
  • Video Recording & Image Capture: Record and capture images directly through the device for review and analysis.
  • USB Video Stream: Stream video output for real-time monitoring or recording.
  • USB-C Interface for External Devices: Connect to external devices for expanded functionality.
  • Inclinometer: Provides precise angle measurements for accurate shooting adjustments.
  • Levelometer: Helps maintain level positioning for improved accuracy.
  • Angle Cosine Indicator: Displays angle cosine values for enhanced targeting precision.
  • Reticle ON/OFF Selection: Enables or disables the reticle display as needed.
  • Battery Status Indicator: Displays remaining battery life for uninterrupted operation.
  • ULTEM Composite Body and Ergonomic Design: Features a durable, lightweight body with user-friendly design for comfortable handling.
  • Designed and Assembled in the USA from U.S. and Global Components: High-quality construction with a focus on domestic craftsmanship.

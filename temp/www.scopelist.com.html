Armasight TAVT36WN9COLL102 Collector 320 1.5-6x 19 mm Gray Thermal Mini Weapon Sight

The Armasight Collector 320 1.5-6x 19 mm Gray Thermal Mini Weapon Sight TAVT36WN9COLL102 combines advanced thermal imaging technology with compact design, ideal for enhancing the performance of firearms like .223/.556 rifles, rimfire weapons, airguns, and crossbows. Engineered with the renowned Iron Wolf thermal technology platform, this Armasight TAVT36WN9COLL102 weapon sight ensures exceptional visual clarity and responsiveness. Its 60Hz frame rate facilitates seamless operation, providing users with a detailed view optimized for typical engagement distances.

This model features a robust suite of functionalities tailored for tactical and hunting applications. It includes multiple color palettes for adapting to various environments, intuitive 3-button controls for effortless operation, and various reticle types and colors to suit different shooting scenarios. The Armasight Collector Weapon Sight also offers convenient features such as video and image capture capabilities, USB video streaming, and a USB-C interface for seamless integration with external devices like computers and televisions. Built with a durable ULTEM composite body and ergonomic design, this sight combines reliability with precision, ensuring it performs exceptionally under diverse conditions.

Features of the Armasight Collector 320 Thermal Weapon Sight:

  • Multiple color palettes
  • Intuitive 3-button controls
  • 8+ Reticle Types
  • Up to 4 hour runtime at 20° C
  • Image Detail Enhancement
  • 1/2MOA Boresight
  • Standard Picatinny Mount Included
  • Video Recording & Image Capture
  • USB Video Stream
  • USB-C Interface for External Devices
  • Inclinometer

Details

The FLIR Breach PTQ136 is a thermal imaging monocular specifically designed for law enforcement applications. With its compact and rugged design, the PTQ136 provides unmatched tactical awareness to officers in the field. Weighing only 7.4 ounces (210 grams), the PTQ136 can easily be concealed in a pocket or mounted to a helmet adding minimal weight to officers heavy gear.

Equipped with the advanced FLIR Boson thermal core and a high definition display, the PTQ136 provides exceptional vision and image clarity, enabling better detection and classification of suspects or evidence, day or night. The monocular also features seven palettes for fast detection of suspects or evidence in any lighting condition.

The PTQ136 is designed for convenient hands-free operation with multiple mini-rail mounting points, allowing for a wide variety of head mounts, helmet mounts, and additional equipment installations. In addition, the device includes a digital compass/inclinometer to enhance situational awareness and help officers navigate in any environment.

The PTQ136 also features onboard recording capabilities and image capture, allowing for easy documentation of operations. With its exceptional vision, hands-free operation, and superior value, the FLIR PTQ136 is an indispensable tool for law enforcement officers who require reliable thermal imaging technology to complete their missions.

Description

The Armasight Collector 320 Thermal Weapon Sight TAVT36WN9COLL102 is a compact and lightweight optic designed for agility and responsiveness with .223/.556, rimfire, airgun, or crossbow platforms. Powered by the ArmaCORE 320x240 thermal core, this unit boasts a fast 60Hz frame rate and exceptional visual acuity based on Armasights Iron Wolf thermal technology. It features video and image capture capabilities, USB video streaming, and a USB-C interface for easy viewing on external devices. With multiple color palettes, intuitive 3-button controls, and over 8 reticle types, this sight offers versatility and ease of use. The Armasight Collector 320 ensures long-lasting performance with its 4-hour runtime, ULTEM composite body, and ergonomic design.

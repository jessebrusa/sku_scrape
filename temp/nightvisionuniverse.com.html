Armasight Collector Compact 320 Thermal Scope 19mm 320x240/12um 60Hz Onboard Recording/WiFi (TAVT36WN9COLL102)

 

The Collector 320’s compact and lightweight design makes it an ideal choice for keeping your .223/.556 rifles, rimfire guns, airguns, or crossbows nimble and responsive. Specifically optimized for the typical ranges these platforms are used at, the Collector 320 delivers smooth performance with its fast 60Hz frame rate and exceptional visual clarity, thanks to Armasight’s Iron Wolf thermal technology platform.

This thermal unit comes equipped with an extensive set of features, including video and image capture, USB video streaming, and a USB-C interface that allows users to view recordings and photos on external devices such as computers or televisions. The Collector 320 also offers multiple color palettes, simple 3-button controls, various reticle types and colors, and extended battery life for long-lasting use.
